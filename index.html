About Us



As a group of friends, we thought about how we took our sanitation for granted. A majority of the world does not have access to soap because there are more issues at hand. Cleanliness is one of the most hidden, but biggest problems that exist across the globe, so in order to give people a hand, we decided made a small startup to create a soap that is accessible and impacting to all.



dot. was founded by a group of former AP Chemistry students that wanted to put their newfound skills to use and benefit their community. They put together the most creative minds to bring you a quality soap that suits your home and lifestyle!


Here we are!



Our mission: To deliver relaxation through a cleanse that appeals in the physical as well as the olfactory sense, all the while keeping pace with the speed of life.
